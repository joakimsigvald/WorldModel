I clarified that:

2. Contradiction with known physics  
3. Experimental incompatibility  

And you said you are willing to **backtrack** or **close the project** if major structural cracks appear.

# 4. Evolution of Your Consciousness Model

You described how your thinking has shifted:

### Earlier:
- Atoms → conscious  
- Electrons/quarks → conscious  
- A parallel hierarchy of *physical* and *mental* building blocks  

You now view this as **too naïve**.

### Current view:
- Level‑0 bits have proto-awareness  
- But higher structures (loops, EM field, photons, atoms) become **rigid architectures**  
- Rigid structures suppress or eliminate any higher‑order agency  
- Physics and chemistry are **Level‑2**, rule-based, deterministic  

### Level‑3 consciousness does **not** arise in physics or chemistry.  
It emerges only when:

- Structures become messy  
- Nonlinear  
- Self-organizing  
- Thermodynamically open  
- Capable of maintaining deviation from equilibrium  

→ Typically at the border of **complex chemistry → early biology**.

So:

- Level‑0 to Level‑2 = proto-awareness only  
- Level‑2 (physics, chemistry) = deterministic, frozen, non-conscious  
- Level‑3 = emergent agency in biological-like systems  
- Higher levels = nervous systems → minds → collective cognition  

This refined ontology is **non-panpsychist**, **structured**, and **emergent**.
